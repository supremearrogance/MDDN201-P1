Antennas

Whether it be through Radio or Television, Antennas enable households to connect and be updated with the latest current events around the world. They work by receiving and transmitting Electro-magnetic Waves to other recievers which inturn creates a signal. Antennas are almost a necessity for most New Zealand Households, which makes it more accessible for Welingtonians to connect to the rest of the World.
