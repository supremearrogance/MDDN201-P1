Antennas

Whether it be through Radio or Television, Antennas enable households to connect and be updated with the latest current events around the world. They work by receiving and transmitting Electro-magnetic Waves to other recievers which inturn creates a signal. Antennas are almost a necessity for most New Zealand Households, which makes it more accessible for Welingtonians to connect to the rest of the World.

Sky Satellite

The Sky satellite enables Sky TV customers to connect to the Sky TV Network and watch specially broadcasted channels through a subscription payment. It allows customers to watch live sports and connect to other overseas channels from the comforts of their own home.

Visit the Sky TV website for more about their broadcasting channels.

UHF Aerial

A UHF Aerial is a transmitter which broadcasts Free-to-Air channels onto Televisions via a TV Tuner.

Refer to the Freeview website for more about this.
